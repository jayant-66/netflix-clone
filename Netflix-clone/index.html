What is Netflix?

Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that allows users to watch a wide variety of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more on internet-connected devices, offering unlimited access for a monthly fee

How much does Netflix cost?

Netflix plans range from $7.99 to $24.99 per month, with options for ad-supported, standard, and premium streaming, and the possibility to add extra members for an additional fee.

How do I Cancel

To cancel your Netflix subscription, sign in to netflix.com, navigate to your account, find the "Membership & Billing" section, and click "Cancel Membership," then "Finish Cancellation".
